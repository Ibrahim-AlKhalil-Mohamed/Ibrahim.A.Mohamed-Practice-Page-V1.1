Text Decoration

Text decoration in CSS offers styling options like underlines, overlines, and line- throughs to enhance text appearance on web pages. These decorations provide visual emphasis and flair to content. See examples below:


  • Line-through: Strikes through text, indicating completed tasks or discounted prices.

  • Overline: Adds a horizontal line above the text, highlighting headings or important info.

  • Underline: Adds a horizontal line beneath the text. Commonly used for links. There are different styles such as
